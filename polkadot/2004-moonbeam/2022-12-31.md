[Schema](https://github.com/colorfulnotion/substrate-etl/blob/main/schema/evmtxs.json)
```bash
SELECT date(block_time) as logDT, 
 COUNT(*) numEVMTransactions
 FROM `substrate-etl.polkadot.evmtxs2004`
```
